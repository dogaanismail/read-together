# Code of Conduct

We are committed to creating a welcoming, safe, and inclusive community for people who stutter and allies.

This project adopts the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1.

- Homepage: https://www.contributor-covenant.org
- Full text: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

In short: be kind, inclusive, and respectful. We are building a safe space for practice and support. Harassment, discrimination, or dismissive behavior will not be tolerated.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:
- Open a private security/advisory discussion if available, or
- Email the maintainer (see https://github.com/dogaanismail for contact details)

We’ll review and respond as promptly as possible, prioritizing community safety and confidentiality.
